## Design Context

### Users

**Gen Z Australian Catholic young people** (16–28). They arrive on phones, scrolling Instagram. They want to know: *Is this for me? What is it? When? Where? How do I sign up?* They've seen plenty of generic SaaS websites and they're allergic to corporate-cheery copy. They respond to:

- Real photos of people who look like them, in worship, in community
- Scripture treated reverently — not as a tagline, as the source
- Strong, confident type and colour, not pastel "wellness" palettes
- Direct CTAs ("Register"), not slick funnel patter ("Don't miss out")

The job-to-be-done is binary: *register and book the dates*, or *send the link to a friend who should*. Everything else is hype-building.

### Brand Personality

**Reverent · Bold · Australian.**

Voice attributes:

- **Invitational, not promotional.** "Come and encounter" / "Look to him". Never "Don't miss out".
- **Scripture-anchored.** Every major surface opens or closes on a verse. The 2026 theme verse is **Psalm 34:5** — *"Look to him, and be radiant; so your faces shall never be ashamed."*
- **First-person plural / second-person singular.** *We / us / our* for the JY community; *you / your* for the reader. Almost never *I*.
- **Imperative mood.** Verbs lead: *Encounter. Come. Register. Look to him.*
- **Reverent capitalisation.** *Him, His, Christ, the Shepherd*. **CHOSEN** all caps in display. *conference* lowercase under it.
- **Australian English.** *evangelise, organise, colour, programme.*

Casing rules:

- Display headlines and theme words → Title Case (Anton).
- Logo lockup → CHOSEN all caps + *conference* lowercase.
- Eyebrow / category labels → ALL CAPS, `letter-spacing: 0.18em`, gold.
- Body → sentence case.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Warm reverence + quiet joy.** Light dawning, not party popper. Posters use a *central radial composition* — rays fanning from a focal sacred image (the monstrance on the *Be Radiant* poster). Recreate that gravity in web layouts.

**Colour theology** — these aren't decoration, they're meaning:

- `--chosen-navy #0B0A55` — the faithful, royalty, the chosen children. **Dominant** for digital surfaces.
- `--chosen-gold #EDA90C` — the Tau cross, the light of truth. **Primary accent** — CTAs, eyebrow labels, scripture cites, gold rules.
- `--chosen-red #F71A1D` — youth beckoning for Christ. Theme-word "Be Radiant", urgency. **Never for body links.**
- `--chosen-royal #4071AC` — God descending from heaven. Secondary surface colour.
- `--chosen-yellow #EBC903` and `--chosen-orange #FE4E0E` — sun ring, radiance.
- `--neutral-50 #FAF8F3` — warm paper. Light surfaces are this, *never pure white*.

**Typography:**

- **Work Sans** — body, UI, navigation, labels. Weights 300–900.
- **Anton** — display/hero headlines ONLY. Tall condensed single-weight. (Substitute for the original poster face — open item: confirm with Irene.)
- **Bebas Neue** — fallback for Anton.
- **No serif typefaces.** Reverence comes from spacing and colour, not classical type.
- **Eyebrow labels** — ALL CAPS, `letter-spacing: 0.18em`, gold, 11px.

**Layout posture:** Generous vertical rhythm. Heroes get 1.5× the space of neighbours. Strong horizontal bands (alternating navy / paper / royal blue) over multi-column complexity. Photographs full-bleed, hard-edged (radius 0). Cards 8px radius. CTAs pill (9999px).

**Photography:** Warm-graded, gold-and-red dominant, deeply saturated. Real Australian Catholic youth in worship, prayer, community. Sacramental imagery (monstrance, host, hands) shot reverently and centrally framed. **Never stock photos. Never desaturated.**

### Anti-references — do NOT do these

- Glassmorphism / blurred-glass cards
- Parallax scrolling
- Bounce / spring / elastic easing
- Generic SaaS dark gradients (purple-to-blue, neon-on-dark)
- Inter / Roboto / Open Sans / Instrument Serif / Archivo Black (the prototype's discarded fonts)
- Centred-everything layouts. Asymmetric is more designed.
- Card-grid monotony — same-sized icon-heading-text cards repeated endlessly
- Hero metric template (big number, small label, gradient accent)
- Gradient text for "impact"
- Pure black `#000` or pure white `#fff` — always tint
- Emoji on official surfaces (social posts may use sparingly to match platform tone, never on event collateral)
- AI-cyan-on-dark, AI-purple-gradient
- Default dark mode with glowing accents

### Design Principles

1. **Photography is the icon set.** No icon library above headings. The brand uses the logo medallion, the radiant rays, and real photography as visual ornament.
2. **Mobile-first, then enrich for larger screens.** Most visitors arrive on a phone. Test mobile viewport every commit.
3. **Animation is reverent.** Fades and gentle ease-out (`cubic-bezier(0.22, 0.61, 0.36, 1)`), 200–400ms. Hero photos can Ken Burns at 15–20s, ≤4% scale. Radiant rays rotate at 60s/turn. Scripture fades in with a 16px upward translate. **All gated on `prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference`.**
4. **Editor UX matters as much as front-end output.** Irene will use this. Block fields need clear labels, sensible defaults, placeholder text, and visible validation.
5. **Tailwind first.** Custom CSS only when utilities aren't enough (radiant-rays motif, Ken Burns keyframe, marquee track, register-pulse).
6. **Server-render anything dynamic.** `render.php`, never `save.js`, for content reading from CPTs/DB or relying on a constant.
7. **Accessibility is non-negotiable.** Semantic HTML, alt text required as block attributes (no submit without it), keyboard nav tested, focus rings visible (`--shadow-glow-gold`), WCAG AA contrast minimum.
