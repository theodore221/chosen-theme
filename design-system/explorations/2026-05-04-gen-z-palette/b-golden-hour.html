A National Gathering

Four days. One mission. Thousands of young Catholics.

CHOSEN is the annual national Catholic youth conference of Jesus Youth Australia. Four days of adoration, talks, workshops, worship, and unfiltered encounter with Christ. Come and get equipped to be a leader with practical, life-changing steps that help you boldly live a life of purpose.
