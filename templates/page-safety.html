Our commitment

[DRAFT] Jesus Youth Australia is committed to the safety, wellbeing, and dignity of every child and young person who attends CHOSEN. We adhere to the National Catholic Safeguarding Standards (NCSS) issued by Catholic Professional Standards Limited. We have zero tolerance for any form of abuse, harassment, or exploitation.

Working With Children Checks

[DRAFT] Every CHOSEN volunteer, leader, speaker, and supervising adult who interacts with delegates under 18 holds a current Working With Children Check (WWCC) from their state of residence. WWCCs are verified before any volunteer is assigned to a role with under-18 contact.

For under-18 delegates

[DRAFT] Delegates aged 16 and 17 attend CHOSEN with parental or guardian consent (collected at registration) and are supervised throughout the conference by trained adult leaders with current WWCCs. Sleeping arrangements, transport, and supervised activities follow the NCSS guidelines for residential events involving minors.

Raising a concern

[DRAFT] If you have a safeguarding concern — at, before, or after the conference — please contact our Safeguarding Lead immediately at safeguarding@chosenconference.org.au. For matters involving the immediate safety of a child, contact your local police on 000 first. Concerns can also be reported to the relevant Catholic diocesan Safeguarding Office.

Code of conduct

[DRAFT] All CHOSEN delegates, volunteers, leaders, and visitors are expected to conduct themselves in a manner consistent with the dignity of every person. Bullying, harassment, discrimination, illicit substances, and inappropriate physical contact are not tolerated. The full code of conduct is provided to delegates at registration.
